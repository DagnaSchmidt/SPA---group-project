Hot stone

Hot Stone massage is a fantastic spa treatment that provides complete relaxation. The lava stones have an ability to retain heat, which increases the effect of maximum relaxation. You will feel soft, warm and de-stressed after the treatment

Facial treatment

This classic facial treatment gives you a moment of relaxation while the skin is cleansed of impurities. Our ambition is to adapt your facial treatment based on your conditions. It begins with a deep cleansing and ends with a customized face mask and a facial massage. Choose between 30, 60 or 75 minutes.

Foot massage

A refreshing foot massage, first you get to enjoy a softening aroma foot bath before we file away calluses on your feet. To get your feet really baby soft, we use a foot scrub before wrapping your feet in a softening wrap. We end the treatment with a short foot massage with beneficial foot cream.

Spa massage

Enjoy a wonderfully relaxing massage with warm oils that soften the muscles and give you peace of mind. Our well-heated treatment beds lets your body relax, forget the daily stress and let yourself be swept away. The spa massage is a full body massage including head and feets.
