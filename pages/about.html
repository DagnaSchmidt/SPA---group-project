About Us

On a sunny day you will see the buildings of Aberdeen shimmer like silver because of its many grey-stone buildings. With victorian architecure and a long, sandy coastline, you'll find Aberdeen on the North East of Scotland, just a few hours train ride from Edinburgh. Throughout the streets and parks you'll discover a citywide floral display which include over sixteen million flowers, everything between roses to daffodils to crocuses. Your travel to the Silvery City Spa will be more than just a place to relax, it will be an opportunity to explore and enjoy.

Find Us

From the dynamic and buzzing city centre you'll find a more comforting part of town just half an hour away; we are located south of the peaceful Hazlehead Park, perfect to visit for a stroll before or after your visit. From the train station you can take bus 17 to Milltimber and jump off at the Garden centre.

Spa Etiquette

Silver City Spa is a place that invites you to switch off from the outside world, relax and recharge. It's a place to rebalance and we encourage you to be considerate of people around you and keep the general noise levels to a minimum. Please refrain from talking loudly and instead use your "spa voice". Please respect the following rules throughout all of the spa, including svimming pools, gym, treatment rooms, and relaxation areas:

  • No phone calls
  • Photography or recording of videos is allowed anywhere
  • Shower before using the facilities
  • No perfume
  • Shaving is not permitted.
